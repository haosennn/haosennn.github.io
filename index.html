About me

My name is Haosen Liu, currently a second-year PhD student in the department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering of the University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Edmund Y. Lam.

My research interest includes image processing and machine learning. I am now studying the weakly supervised learning for image restoration.
